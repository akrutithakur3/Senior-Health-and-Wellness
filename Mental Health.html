Mental Health

As we age, taking care of our mental health is just as important as taking care of our physical health. Here are some resources to help you maintain good mental health.

Mental Health Tips

  • Stay active and engaged in your community
  • Stay connected with loved ones
  • Practice stress-reducing activities such as meditation or yoga
  • Consider therapy or counseling if you are struggling with your mental health
